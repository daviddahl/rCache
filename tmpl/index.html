Researchers need to be organized.

All of their meticulously researched data needs to accessible 24 x 7.
Bookmarks may have been a good way to organize online information in the past, but linked resources tend to disappear:

Sometimes these resources disappear altogether from the web, and are not even stored in search engine caches. This can be frustrating and waste valuable time.

rCache: your personal search repository.

  • rCache is a powerful tool that lets you archive data from many unrelated sources.
  • rCache is a web scraper, a bookmarking tool and more.
  • rCache can extract text from Word and PDF documents.
  • Tag-based lookups as well as keyword searches can be performed on your data.
  • rCache is for the serious researcher.
  • Create accounts for your Colleagues to give limited or full access to your Entries.
  • rCache is that always-there place to store data that you want to refer back to.
  • rCache works hand in glove with the best of breed web browser, Firefox™
